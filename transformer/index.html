The Transformer

The Transformer is the architecture behind GPT, Claude, and every modern language model. Introduced in the 2017 paper "Attention Is All You Need," it revolutionized AI by processing entire sentences in parallel rather than word by word.

This visualization walks you through the architecture step by step — with tiny vectors you can follow by hand — from an input sentence all the way to a next-word prediction.

  • Follow 10 steps: tokenization, embedding, attention, feed-forward, and prediction
  • See the actual matrix operations with small, readable numbers
  • Understand why attention lets the model capture relationships between all words at once
